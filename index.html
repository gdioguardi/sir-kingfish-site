WHO WE ARE

Sir Kingfish is an Ottawa-based band established in 2017 by four musicians: Kyle Maundrell, Maxx Brennan, Ronnie Cramp-Sharp, and Grant Dioguardi. With Sir Kingfish producing rock, metal, punk, and jazz music, the eclectic band is not easily defined by any one genre.
